Caramelized Chicken Wings

A Caramelized Chicken Wings looks sticky and juicy. It is famous by it milky tasty, its delicious aroma, and spicy touch.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Black Peeper
  • Tomato
  • chive
  • Chilli Sauce
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Season the wings with salt and peeper
  2. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes
  3. Mix the milk with some Avocado oil
  4. Add a spoon of chive, a spoon of oregano, two spoons of black peeper, four spoons of chilli sauce
  5. When the wings finish baking mix it with the sauce
  6. Put back the mixture in oven for 5 minutes
