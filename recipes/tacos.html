Beef Tacos

Beef Tacos is made of some stake a wrapped with tortillas; marinated with onions, garlic, fresh tomato, black peeper, chilli flacks, and beef seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Peeper
  • Chilli Flacks
  • Beef seasonings

Steps

  1. Season the beef and let it sit for 30 minutes
  2. Grill the meet for 15 minutes
  3. Cook the meet on a pan with 2 tea spoons of olive oil
  4. Add the rest of the Ingredients and cook for 5 minutes
  5. Toast the Tortillas and wrap the cooked meet in to it
